--------------------
For proper operation and maintenance, the following instruments and apparatus shall be provided for each generator panel:  
1 - Air circuit breaker (ACB)  
1 - AC ammeter  
1 - Change-over switch for AC ammeter (for each phase of generator)  
1 - AC voltmeter  
1 - Change-over switch for AC voltmeter (between each line of generator)  
1 – Power factor meter  
1 – Running hour meter  
1 - Frequency meter  
1 - Manual voltage regulator.  
1 - ACB open/close control switch (or push button)  
1 - Switch for space heater  
1 - Generator running indicating lamp (white)  
1 - ACB close indicating lamp (green)  
1 - ACB open indicating lamp (red)  
1 - Space heater indicating lamp (orange)  
1 - Reverse power relay  
1 - Running hour meter  
1 – Diesel gen. remote control ready indicating lamp  
1 –set of diesel gen. start/stop switch  
1 – set of power management unit  
1 – set of differential protection transformer (supplied by manufacturer)  
**1 – set standby indicator light in blue**  
1- Switch for External excitation and De Excitation  
Etc.  
And other necessary equipment;  
***The generators system must automatically block start of Bow Thruster till the load is brought down to acceptable limits.***
