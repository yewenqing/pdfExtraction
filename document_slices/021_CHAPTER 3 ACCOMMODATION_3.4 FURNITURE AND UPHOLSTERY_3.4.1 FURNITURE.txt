#### 3.4.1.1 General  
The furniture and fittings shall be of strong construction for shipboard using and in harmony with decorative treatment of the space and shall be in accordance with the Builder’s practice.  
Main furniture and fittings shall be as described in detail and the size and number of furniture may be slightly changed by the Cabin Arrangement Plan.  
***All beds shall be arranged in direction of longitudinal and sofas in private rooms shall be arranged in direction of transverse as far as possible. If not they must be in opposite direction.***  
***In Cabins and Public areas where entertainment appliances are provided must be supplied from individual power socket. An Extra power socket should also be provided at each area***.  
The general features and size of furniture shall be as listed up in the following. Color scheme book shall be submitted to Buyer for approval.  
The surface material of furniture in general shall be as follows.  
|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Item | Material |
| Steel | Spray paint or chromium plating |
| Wood | Polyurethane finish laminated sheets |
| Chair | Wood (Living cabin, Conference room) |
| Fixed revolving steel chair (mess room) | Steel |  
In general the furniture to be constructed of the plywood/chipboard cover with decoration plate and trimmed with high quality hardwood in all cabin classes and formaldehyde level to satisfy Chinese Furniture Standard melamine film in visible part only.  
Top of all tables and other horizontal top surfaces to be finished with melamine laminate.  
Chairs and movable sofas shall be provided with fastening device. Other furniture shall be fixed to wall, deck and/or ceiling.

#### 3.4.1.3 Furniture  
**The list below is for information. The final amount and arrangement to be according to cabin’s arrangement which approved by buyer.**  
One (1) ***full size*** mirror to be provided in the wardrobe.  
***All drawers and doors of furniture’s to be fitted with snap locks. Locks shall be provided for wardrobes and the uppermost tier of drawer for desk.***  
***All beds shall be provided with two (2) drawers underneath (except for bed in hospital).***  
***Heavy furniture normally will be standing type. If heavy furniture only mounting on wall reinforce plate will pre install inside the lining.***  
***All edges of furniture must be rounded off with no sharp edges.***  
***Life jacket and immersion suit to be fitted in a dedicated shelf in wardrobe.***  
***All refrigerators should be using environmentally friendly gas.***
