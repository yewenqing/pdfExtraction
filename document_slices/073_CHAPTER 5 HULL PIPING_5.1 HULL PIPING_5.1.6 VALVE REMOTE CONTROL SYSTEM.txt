1) Valve remote operation  
The valve control system of pneumatic type shall be provided for the remote control of following valves:  
- Ballast valves in cargo hold area and major valves in engine room for ballast system  
- Bilge suction valves in cargo hold area  
- Fuel oil filling / suction main valves in cargo hold area  
Discharge main valves of the ballast pumps shall have open, closed and intermediated position indications. Other valves shall have open and closed indication only.  
The remote operated suction valves for HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO bunker tanks in cargo hold area shall be single acting type.  
The remotely controlled valves shall have an emergency operation facility by means of portable hand pump or spanner.  
**Remote valves should not be placed in areas they serve and should be easily accessible in any operational condition of vessel. where is possible.**  
2) Control  
A control station with color graphic monitor shall be provided in the ship office, engine control room for ballast, bilge, FO.  
Control functions and indications mentioned below shall be mounted on the monitor.  
- Mimic diagram of ballast, bilge and FO system  
- Open / shut function with indication for remote operated valves (Remote control valves in fuel oil system shall be controlled from engine control room.)  
- Start/ stop function with running indication for ballast pump, fire, bilge & G.S. pumps.  
- Suction and discharge pressure indication on monitor for ballast pump  
- Level indication able to display tank volumes on monitor for water ballast, fresh water tank, HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO storage tanks and MGO storage tank.  
- Draft indication on monitor  
- High level alarm for cargo hold bilge wells, bosun store, fore & aft end of pipe duct, bow thruster & emergency fire pump room.  
- Other necessary equipment and fittings not mentioned above shall be provided as per maker's standard  
- HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO and MGO storage tanks shall be provided with independent level switches for high level alarm.
