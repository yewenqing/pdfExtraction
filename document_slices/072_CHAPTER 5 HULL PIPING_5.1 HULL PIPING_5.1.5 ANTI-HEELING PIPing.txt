Two (2) anti-heeling tanks (P&S) shall be cross connected by steel pipes and one (1) dedicated anti-heeling pump shall be provided.  
During loading and unloading of containers, heel adjustments shall be carried out by means of transferring the sea water between one pair of side ballast tank (port and starboard).  
The anti-heeling operation shall be effected by start and stop sequence of heeling pump and respective control of valve and shall be controlled the Vessel's heel within +/-2 degrees.  
Heeling control system shall have the start/stop function for heel adjusting, the heel indication and the over heeling alarm for heel adjusting tanks on the heeling control mode on TFT in ship office.  
Before loading and unloading of containers, both anti-heeling tanks shall be about half filled with sea water.  
Anti-heeling Pump  
No. of set : One (1)  
Capacity : About 350 m3/h x 15 mTH (according to final calculation)  
Type : Reversible propeller type  
Prime mover : Electric motor  
Materials : Pump casing of bronze, impeller of Ni-Al-bronze and shaft of stainless steel  
The following alarm shall be provided visible on the display of the operator panel and audible at the remote control panel:  
Excessive Heeling (>5 degree)/ Emergency stop  
Failure heeling pump and relative valves  
Heeling tanks level alarm  
The effect at any alarm occurring shall result in closing of valves and stopping of pump.  
System common abnormal alarm to AMS shall be provided
