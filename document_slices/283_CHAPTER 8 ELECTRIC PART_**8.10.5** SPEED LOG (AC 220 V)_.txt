--------------------
The speed log must meet the requirement of Resolution MSC.334 (90).  
***(Option:Speed log on the bridge display screen, there are speed of water or speed of ground, were displayed the direction of flow and arrows of velocity indicator.)***  
One (1) set of Doppler speed log shall be provided as follows:  
1- Transducer with gate valve fitted in ship forward bottom  
1- Electronic unit in fore room  
1- Main unit in chart space  
**1**- Speed indicator at wheelhouse ceiling console  
The speed signal shall be sent to two radars, AIS and VDR and autopilot etc.
