One (1) complete set of adaptive auto-pilot shall be installed consisting of:  
One (1) - Steering stand in the wheelhouse, which might be combined with the bridge consoles;  
The steering stand shall be equipped with a steering mode select switch in order to select the operation mode of automatic steering or electric hand steering with follow-up or non follow-up control. Non follow-up control units for both bridge wings shall be provided.  
The steering stand shall be provided with power failure alarm and off course alarm.  
The feedback unit shall be arranged in steering gear room with level link rod as per the makerâ€™s standard .  
Auto pilot override tiller to be provided in main console on wheelhouse.  
The heading signal from the magnetic compass and gyro compass shall be connect in the autopilot separately.
