--------------------
In general, class “B” or “F” insulation shall be adopted, except special service motors for which insulation are in accordance with the manufacturer's standard.  
In general, all motors shall be provided with waterproof terminal box of cast iron or steel plate construction with gland and removable cover. All terminals shall be marked.
