--------------------
The intermediate shaft shall be made of forged high quality carbon steel with integral flanges at both ends*,* and coupled together with each shaft with parallel forged steel reamer bolts.  
The shaft shall be machined all over and finished brightly in way of bearing where the diameter is increased abt.5mm. The shafting shall be electrically earthen to the hull; a shaft earthing device shall be fitted to the intermediate shaft.
