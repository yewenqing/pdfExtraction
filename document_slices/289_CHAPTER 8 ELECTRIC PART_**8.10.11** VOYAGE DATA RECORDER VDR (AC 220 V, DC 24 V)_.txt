--------------------
One (1) set of voyage data recorder system VDR which performance standard will meet the requirement of Resolution MSC.333 (90) shall be provided as follows:  
One (1) main electronics unit for data acquisition and recording shall be installed in the suitable space of bridge deck.  
One (1) capsule (black box, auto floating type) ***and*** One (1) capsule (black box, fixed type) shall be fixed on compass deck and shall be capable of being removed but secure against tampering.  
Interface box and power supply unit shall be provided. ***The data shall be easy download without any special needs to a hard drive and can be play back on a normal PC.***  
Six (6) sets of microphones to be provided, two (2) outdoor type microphones on both bridge wings and four (4) indoor type microphones in the wheelhouse.  
VDR extension backup cable for captain day room computer will be provided and the computer shall be supplied by ***Owner***.  
Gyro compass, speed log, echo sounder, at least 2 stations radar video, DGPS, rudder order, engine order, wind speed and direction, VHF communication audio, alarm and other information requirement by rules shall be sent to the VDR.
