--------------------
***Flame retardant and*** non-corrosive material are to be used for nameplates, labels, etc. ***Nameplates on open decks shall be made of brass or stainless steel***.  
All nameplates are to be fixed with screws or rivets. ***The nameplates shall include at least item number and functional description***  
Type of plate shall be as follows:  
|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| - | Rotating machine | : | ***Flame retardant and non***-corrosive metal plate |
| - | Controller and panel | : | Plastic plate or Maker’ standard, except  non-corrosive metal plate for those exposed to weather |
| - | Others | : | Maker’s standard |  
The letter on the nameplate shall be in English. The fitting type of nameplate should be screw fitting or according to maker’s standard.  
The caution plates shall be in English.
