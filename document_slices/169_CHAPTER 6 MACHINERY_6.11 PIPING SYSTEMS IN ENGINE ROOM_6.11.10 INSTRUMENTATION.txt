Following instrument shall be provided for each machinery or on the piping side, and exclusive of the instruments used for automatic control in ECR.  
#### 6.11.10.1 Pressure, Compound and Vacuum Gauges  
Pressure, compound and vacuum gauges shall be manufactured in accordance with the Chinese Industrial Standard or equivalent for marine use.  
All gauge to have black figures, white faces, letter in English and units in metric or SI system (Red figures for vacuum), and working range shall be clearly marked for each gauge. Following gauges shall be furnished.
