--------------------
The Vessel to be designed for carrying ISO standard container of the types stated in Section 2.2 of “General Part” of the Specification.  
Lashing and securing system shall ensure safe carriage of containers of all variations of high and low cube containers as per the requirement of Rules and Regulations.  
Cargo Safe Access Plan (CSAP) in accordance with MSC.1/Circ.1352and MSC.1/Circ. 1353 to be prepared by the Builder.  
All container loose fittings and Container Securing Manual (CSM) required by IMO shall be provided by the Buyer.  
***The loose twist stackers supplied by the Buyer for mixed stowage shall be stowed in the***  
***dedicated storage bins and racks supplied by the Buyer on upper deck***
