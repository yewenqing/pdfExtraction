1. Engine control room (ECR)  
Engine control room surrounded with steel walls shall be sound insulated and air conditioned.  
In the ECR the following equipment shall be provided:  
- Main switchboard  
* Engine control console; One monitor for Valve remote control system along with tank level remote gauge shall be combined.  
- Uninterrupted Power Supply unit for automation system (UPS)  
* Auto telephone and sound power telephone  
- Electronic modules and operator panels for M/E remote control system  
- ECR manual control panel of M/E  
- Fire alarm repeater panel  
- Air condition unit  
- Remote valve operation and tank sounding shall be possible from ECR and ship office.  
- Other necessary furniture specified elsewhere  
2. Ship office  
In the ship office the following equipment shall be provided:  
- Ballast control station  
- Auto. telephone  
- AMS Extend alarm panel  
- Reefer container monitoring panel  
- Loading computer  
* 1 pcs of 24 inch TFT VDU for ballast control and level measure system
* Anti-heeling system control
* Ballast water treatment system panel
* Cargo hold fans remote control and running indicator
