--------------------
The following device shall be provided for AC 220 V feeder panel:  
Necessary quantity - Miniature circuit breakers (MCB, with abt. 10% spare breakers based on amount of circuit breakers on feed panel);  
1 - Insulation monitor with alarm lamp for checking AC 220 V system  
1 - AC ammeter  
1 - Change-over switch for AC ammeter (for each phase of power transformer secondary circuit)  
1 - AC voltmeter  
1 - Change-over switch for AC voltmeter (between each phase of power transformer secondary circuit)  
And other necessary equipment;
