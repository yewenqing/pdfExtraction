--------------------
In case that the contents in the following each panel of this section differ from manufacturer's standard for specific equipment, Manufacturer's standard shall be applied in general.  
Emergency switchboard shall be installed in emergency generator room.  
In principle, the construction of the emergency switchboard shall be similar to the main switchboard, but service access at front.  
The emergency generator shall be protected by a three-pole air circuit breaker (ACB), which to be of draw-out type.  
Interlock circuit shall be provided between emergency generator ACB and the space heater circuit.  
**A socket of portable welding machine shall be supplied in the ECR, which shall be powered from the ESB.**
