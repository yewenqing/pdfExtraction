#### 3.5.2.1 Material  
Private toilet and hospital toilet shall be of prefabricated unit type.  
Wash basin shall be of white vitreous china for toilet.  
All wash basins shall be provided with hot and cold mixing valve with one (1) outlet. An S-shaped drainage trap shall be fitted.  
Water closet to be of gravity type and fitted with flush valve, and seat and cover to be of plastic material.  
Bathtub shall be constructed of plastic or white china and fitted with shower, hot and cold water mixing valve and drain plug shall be provided.  
Shower shall have flexible hose, and fitted with hot and cold water mixing valve, and the angle of shower head shall be adjustable.  
Stop valves shall be provided for each hot and cold water pipe below wash basin.  
Paper holder, storm rail, towel rail and soap dish shall be provided for individual use as necessary.  
#### 3.5.2.2 Fittings in Lavatory and Changing Room  
Private lavatory Shower set, water closet, wash basin and toilet cabinet with mirror, paper holder, soap dish, towel bar, hand grip, coat/hat hook  
Public toilet /Stevedore’s toilet Water closet, washbasin and lavatory mirror, paper holder, soap dish, towel bar, hand grip, coat/hat hook  
Hospital lavatory Bath tub, water closet, wash basin and toilet cabinet with mirror, paper holder, soap dish, towel bar, handgrip, coat/hat hook
