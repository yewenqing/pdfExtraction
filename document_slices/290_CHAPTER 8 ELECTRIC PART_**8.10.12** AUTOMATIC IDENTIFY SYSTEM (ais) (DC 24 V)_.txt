--------------------
One (1) set of AIS with necessary accessories shall be provided.  
One (1) set antenna shall be installed on compass deck and one (1) controller shall be installed in the wheelhouse.  
One (1) interface for communicating between AIS and pilot’s computer shall be provided, and one (1) power receptacle with supply AC 110 V (USA type socket) for pilot’s computer shall also be provided.  
AIS shall interface with Radar and ECDIS units to display the AIS information on Radar’s TFT and ECDIS display.  
Gyro and DGPS signal and other information requirement by rules shall be sent to AIS. The system may be fed from DC 24 V and AC 220 V.
