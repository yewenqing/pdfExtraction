--------------------
Switches in living quarters shall be of non-water-proof type, and switches in the machinery spaces, galley, laundry, toilets etc. shall be water-proof type in general.  
Switches shall be constructed for wall mounting in general, and flush type switches shall be provided for the spaces where woody linings are fitted.  
The switch for cold provision stores lighting shall be with a red indicating lamp to indicate when the inside lights are on.  
Switches for berth lights and desk lights shall be fitted on their own fixtures.  
In general, all tumbler switches and rotary switches for lighting branch circuits shall be of two-pole type.
