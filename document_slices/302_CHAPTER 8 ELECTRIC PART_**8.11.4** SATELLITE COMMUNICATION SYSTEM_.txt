(1) INMARSAT-Feet Broadband FB 500 (AC 220 V)  
One (1) set INMARSAT Fleet broad band 500 standard will be installed, consisting of:  
1- Main unit  
1- Telephone (in captain day room)  
1- Facsimile in radio space  
1- Antenna on compass deck  
1- AC/DC power supply unit  
(2) INMARSAT-C (AC 220 V / DC 24 V)  
Two (2) sets of standard-C satellite communication SES with EGC reception (One of them may be combined with LRIT and SSAS function), each one shall be provided as follows:  
1-Main unit  
1-Terminal unit  
1-Printer  
1. Antenna  
DC 24 V Power shall be fed from the radio battery.
