--------------------
Two (2) sets of DGPS navigator with necessary accessories shall be provided: one set in wheelhouse control console and one set on the chart table.  
Each 1 - Main display unit in chart space and on WHC  
2 - Antenna on the after mast or compass deck  
1 - Signal distribution box in wheelhouse  
The GPS signal shall be sent to the following equipment: Inmarast-Fleet broadband , Inmarast-C, MF/HF DSC, VHF DSC, Radars, speed log, Echo sounder, VDR , gyro-compass,ballast water treatment, scrubber and other systems requirement etc.
