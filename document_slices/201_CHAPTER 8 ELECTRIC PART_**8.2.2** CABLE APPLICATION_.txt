--------------------
All cables accepted by the Classification Society shall be provided.  
In general, electric cable shall be of IEC marine cable (IEC 60092 series) ,and flame retardant type tested according to IEC 60332-1 and IEC 60332-3 Category, low smoke and halogen-free or equivalent type that has been approved by the Classification Society, except special cable which including: coaxial cable, compensate cable and LAN net cable etc.Cables in accommodation spaces to be of XLPE insulated, Metallic halide free sheathed (extruded or lapped).  
Cables shall be of EP rubber or XLPE insulated, metal wire braided with polyolefin (PO) or polychloroprene rubber (PCP) covering.  
Cables for lighting system in accommodation area shall be of EP rubber or XLPE insulated with polyolefin (PO) polychloroprene rubber (PCP)) covering.  
For portable appliances, cables to be of flexible rubber insulated and polyvinyl chloride (PVC) or polychloroprene rubber (PCP) sheathed, and for table lamps to be of flexible vinyl cord.  
The other insulating material cables may also be adopted, but permissive operating temperatures of cable conductor should be 90 oC.
