--------------------
The propeller shaft shall be made of forged high quality carbon steel with a solid flange coupling on fore end. The propeller shaft shall be smoothly machined all over and finished brightly in way of bearings where the diameter is increased abt. 5mm.  
The after portion of the shaft shall be machined to 1/20 tap to fit the propeller hub and the end of the shaft shall be left-hand threaded to suit a propeller shaft nut.  
Removal of the propeller shaft from the Vessel shall be carried out via a temporary opening cut in the shell plating when overhauling and the location shall be clearly marked. The location of temporary opening shall be given on approval drawing.  
**Eye plates for lifting propeller and rudder to be fitted at stern.**
