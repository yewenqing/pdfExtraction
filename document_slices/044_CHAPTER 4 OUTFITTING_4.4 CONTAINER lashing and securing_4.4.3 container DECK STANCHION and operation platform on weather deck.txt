--------------------
Containers stowed on the outermost sides shall be supported by hatch covers and container deck stanchions as shown on the G.A. plan.  
Container deck stanchion shall be of fabricated box type steel construction, and shall be designed based on the same stack load as the hatch covers.  
Vertical ladder or hand grips and steps to be provided for access from weather deck to top of the deck stanchion.  
Fixed handrail to be provided where necessary.  
***The deck stanchion shall be designed to ensure clear height of passage way from upper deck to bottom of first tier container on hatch cover to be min. 2,000 mm.*** ***Non-skid paint to be applied along the walkway with skirting of contrast color.***  
Transverse working platform shall be provided between cargo hold: Width of clear passageway on working platform: 600mmin general. Height to beat the same level as the top of hatch covers.  
Removable handrails and galvanized steel rope with vinyl coating on both side of working platform to be provided,  
Access to working platform shall be provided through a vertical ladder from upper deck.  
Reefer container socket to be fitted on the hatch coaming and weather protected (IP56)
