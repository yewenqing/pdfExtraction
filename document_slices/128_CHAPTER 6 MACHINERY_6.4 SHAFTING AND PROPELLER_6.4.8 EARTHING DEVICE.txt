An earthling device with shaft potential monitoring at intermediate shaft shall be provided which is capable of keeping the electrostatic voltage between crankshaft and hull below 50mV finally according to the M/E requirement.  
**A digital shaft / hull mili-voltmeter with alarm connected to AMS to be provided.**
