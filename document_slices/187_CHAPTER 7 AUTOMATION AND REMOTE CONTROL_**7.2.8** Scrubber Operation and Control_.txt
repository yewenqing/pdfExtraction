--------------------
The operation and control system shall be the type of programmable logic controller (PLC) Following parameters shall be continuously monitoring for the cleaned exhaust gas and water emissions:  
* Wash water turbidity, pH value, etc. of outlet;
* CO2/SO2 analysis measuring for exhaust gas outlet
* ME/AE power signal and GPS signal shall be supplied by Yard for logging
* etc.  
An operating station shall be in the ECC for the system operation.
