The engine shall be completed with necessary fittings and accessories according to main engine Maker’s standard and the main body shall be included as follows:  
1- Set of high efficiency exhaust turbo-charger**.**  
2- Sets of electronically controlled auxiliary blowers complete with starters **for continuous running according to maker’s standard. Provide the lub. oil pump running to interlocked with the ME auxiliary blower.**  
1- Set of air cooler with cleaning and drain pipes.  
1- Set of turning gear with electric motor, starter and portable switch box with capture cable of 20m and pneumatic interlocking apparatus.  
1- Air starting system.  
1- Set of steam tracer piping for F.O. pipes on the engine.  
1- Set of cleaning device for turbo charger. (According to turbo charger maker's standard)  
1- Set of brackets for the engine platforms with gratings, handling and steps.  
1- Set of steam connection for fire extinguish system of scavenging air space.  
1- Set of pressure regulating valves for fuel oil return.  
1-Set of oil mist detecting device.  
1-Set of drain box for fuel oil leakage alarm  
1- Set of integrated axial damper mounted at the forward end of the crankshaft  
1- 2nd order moment compensator (if necessary), and the type of the compensator shall be according to the M/E maker 's standard.  
1- Torsional vibration damper (if necessary subject to TV calculation and Rules)  
1- Set of friction type engine stays  
***1- Set of bearing wear monitoring***  
***1- Set of Water in oil detection***  
***1- Spare CCUs to be provided***  
***1- The M/E hydraulic power lubricating oil system for FIVA and bearing lubricating system must be independent circuits and not connected to same source***  
***Maker to provide data analysis support during guarantee period.***  
***Turbo charger selection will be confirmed with Owner.***  
ME components (rings, piston skirts, liners, exhaust valves, air cooler etc.) to be according to licensor's latest design recommendation.  
***The spare of the main engine shall be according to maker standard and Class requirement.***  
Revolution indicator and revolution counter (refer to Chapter 7, Automation 7.1.4.3.4)  
Remark: If above fitting and accessories of main engine is not consistent with main engine standard scope range, maker standard shall prevail.
