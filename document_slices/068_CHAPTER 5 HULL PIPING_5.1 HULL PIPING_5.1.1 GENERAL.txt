Description of this section shall generally cover piping system located outside the engine room, unless otherwise specified.  
Materials and dimensions of pipes, joints, valves including bolts, nuts, etc. shall be manufactured in accordance with the Chinese Industrial Standard, and/or the Builder’s Standard except otherwise specified hereinafter, the diameter of connecting flange and distance between flanges of valves shall be in accordance with ISO standard.  
The other recognized industrial standards than mentioned above may be applied to meet the requirements of the Classification Society.  
Valves, pipes and fittings mounted on its equipment shall be in accordance with the manufacturer’s standard.  
All overboard discharges shall be kept clear of pilot ladder, accommodation ladder, draft marking, load line marking and lifeboat launching area as far as practical.  
Piping shall be designed to allow for stress due to thermal expansion and deflection of the Vessel’s structure.  
No ballast pipes or vents for ballast tanks shall pass through oil tanks or vice versa, in general.
