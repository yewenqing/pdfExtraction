--------------------
Oily bilge separator shall be provided for handling the oily bilge water in engine room.  
The performance is to meet the requirements of MARPOL & IMO MEPC 107 (49). The oil content of treated water shall be less than 15 ppm and oil content bilge alarm shall be provided. And T-valve with automatic function shall be used for return water to bilge water holding tank.  
The construction, materials, accessories, spares and tools shall be in accordance with Maker's standard.  
An oil content discharge monitoring shall be installed at outlet of clean bilge discharge pump.  
***Give the capacity of treating the oil in water in the Oil water separator technical agreement.***
