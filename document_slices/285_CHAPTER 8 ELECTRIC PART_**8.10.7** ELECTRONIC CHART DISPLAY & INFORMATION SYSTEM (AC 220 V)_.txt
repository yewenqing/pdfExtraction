--------------------
Electronic chart display & information system (ECDIS) will fully comply with the latest standards and resolutions of IMO, IHO, and IEC. The electronic chart is compatible with ENC (S57 Edition 3) charts, C-MAP, ARCS charts etc. The electronic chart can be overlaid with a variety of navigation data such as radar images, position, heading, speed, and others to facilitate safe and efficient navigation.  
Two (2) independent set of ECDIS (master & back-up) shall be provided; (Option:both ECDIS were installed the Uninterruptible Power System(UPS)}

They shall be supplied via navigation power distribution box which to be fed from MSB and ESB auto-changeable.  
The ECDIS shall have following main functions:  
Electronic chart;  
Radar image superimposition;  
Continuous monitoring vessel position;  
Route planning and route monitoring etc.  
Display of ECDIS to be 24".  
External signals from echo sounder, anemometer, AIS, DGPS, gyrocompass, speed log and radars etc. shall be received.
