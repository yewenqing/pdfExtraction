1.2.1 GENERAL ARRANGEMENT  
The Vessel to be designed and constructed as a full cellular container carrier for unrestricted world-wide ocean going service (except NOx ECA Tier III area and other areas which are stated or limited by the rules and regulation in section 1.3.3, the design and equipment condition of the specification), suitable for carrying dry cargo containers, reefer containers and specified dangerous cargo in closed containers within the scope of the Specification.  
The vessel to be an ocean-going gearless container vessel with a single-acting two stroke turbo charged diesel engine, a fixed pitch propeller and a semi-spade rudder. .  
The vessel is not service for NOx ECA (Tier III) area and do not comply with the rules and regulations regarding NOx Tier III. The vessel shall be built on the basis of Tier II regulation with the certificate of International Air Pollution Protection Certificate (IAPP) and Statement of compliance with the NOx technical code for main & aux. engine (EIAPP) in compliance with Tier II criterion.  
**Open loop scrubber to be provided to satisfy the requirements of Global Sulfur Cap 2020 (option requirement of SECA).**  
**HFO (< 3.5% Sulphur), LSHFO (< 0.5% Sulphur)/ULSFO (< 0.1% Sulphur) and MGO(< 0.1% Sulphur) to be loaded on board.**  
**ULSFO (< 0.1% Sulphur), MGO (< 0.1% Sulphur) shall be adopted for on SOx Emission Control Area (SECA).**  
The vessel to have a continuous main deck with a detached forecastle, eight (8) tiers of deck house.  
The propulsion machinery and living quarters including navigation bridge shall be located semi-aft as shown on the General Arrangement Plan (hereinafter called as the "G.A. plan").  
Containers, cargo holds, ballast water tanks, fuel oil tanks, fresh water tanks, bow thruster room, steering gear room and engine room etc. shall be arranged as shown on the G.A. plan.  
The Vessel shall have four (4) cargo holds. each cargo hold have two (2) hatches each.  
The double hull and double bottom space shall be arranged as ballast water tanks or void space. Fore peak tank shall be designed as ballast tank. Aft peak tank shall be designed asballast tank. One pair of anti-heeling tanks to be arranged at midship area port and starboard sides as shown on GA plan.  
Double hull construction shall be provided for all cargo holds. A double bottom shall be fitted extending from the collision bulkhead to the aft peak bulk head **.**  
**Cargo holds to be designed to have access from upper deck to cargo hold walkways and bilge wells in the aft end. Stringer to be provided as walkway location for each 8’6’’ standard container tiers in hold.**  
One (1) pipe duct shall be provided in double bottom from aft end of No.2 cargo hold compartment to fore end of engine room. ***Small hatch covers in place in fore and aft end of pipe duct.***  
Pipe duct to be fitted with trolley and sufficient lighting to be also provided.  
One (1) set of bow thruster to be installed in bow thruster room..  
Mooring facilities shall be arranged as shown on the G.A. plan.

The design service speed is about18.5 knots**,** based on:  
* On design draught 8.50 m
* Main Engine running at CSR (90% SMCR)
* With 15% sea margin
* Clean hull
* In open, deep sea with Beaufort 0, Douglas sea state 0  
Energy saving design:  
The rudder bulb to be designed optimized together with propeller. The hull lines, propeller and rudder with rudder bulb to be finally decided by towing tank test.  
1.2.8 m/e FUEL CONSUMPTION  
The designed M/E Daily fuel oil consumption (D.F.O.C.) is about 35.69t，based on:  
The Brake Specified Fuel Consumption (B.S.F.C) of the main engine is about 159.0 grams/kWh (excluding M/E supplier’s tolerance) at CSR（9342kW） based on the use of fuel oil with L.C.V. of 42,700 kJ/kg at ISO 3046-1:2002(E) and ISO 15550:2002(E) ambient condition and fulfilling the IMO NOx emission limitation Tier II (ME back pressure max. 60mbar), which shall be determined by shop test.  
Only Brake Specified Fuel Consumption (B.S.F.C) of the main engine based on above-mentioned conditions determined by shop test shall be Contract guaranteed.  
In case the shop test conditions are different from the above, the results shall be corrected in accordance with the manufacture’s standards.
