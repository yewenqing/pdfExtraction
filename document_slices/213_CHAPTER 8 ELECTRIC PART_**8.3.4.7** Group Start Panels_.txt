--------------------
The starters for the essential pumps and ventilators in the engine room shall be assembled into group starter panels. Each unit (fixed type) to have circuit breaker, AC 440V/220V transformer, contactor, thermal relay, indicating pilot, push buttons, ammeter and running hour meter etc. which to be of fixed type.  
Automation change-over function shall be provided for primary essential duplicate pumps.  
The exact item and layout of starter unit shall be determined later during detail design.  
**Following items to be agreed for detail design:**  
**The main switch is to be lockable by pad lock (to inhibit start).**  
**When starter is not visible at the motor, a separate start/stop switch shall be installed near the motor.**
