--------------------
A steam line for HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO bunker tank heating and a drain line in side void space (P or S) shall be led from engine room to fuel oil bunker tanks and FO overflow tank.  
Heating coils shall be of seamless steel and heating area ratio shall be 0.06m2/m3 for heavy fuel oil bunker tanks.  
The steam shall be led from steam supply main line in void space through under deck to each HFO/ULSFO tank via a manually operated shut-off valve, and led back to drain main line in the void space through a steam trap/strainer and a shut-off valve .  
Steam tracing shall be adopted for suction pipe of HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO line and steam tracing shall be insulated together with HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO suction main line.  
***The remote temperature reading shall be provided for the HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO bunker tanks.***
