The following fixed fire-fighting systems shall be provided for the spaces as listed below.  
Sea water system Exposed decks, Engine room, Steering gear room, Cargo hold, Paint store  
Living quarters, and other working spaces  
Fixed fresh water mist system Localized fire suppression in E/R  
CO2 fixed system Engine room, Cargo hold, Galley hood  
Portable fire extinguisher Living quarters and other working spaces, engine room, emergency generator room and emergency fire pump room  
All fire-fighting system and equipment shall comply with the requirements of SOLAS.  
Fire Control Plan shall be provided in accordance with Rule and Regulation and shall be stored in galvanized steel case on both sides of living quarters on upper deck.  
Fire control station is shall be provided with controls for quick closing valves, E/R fans, CO2 releasing box for engine room, oil pumps etc.  
***One fire detector for every laundry.***
