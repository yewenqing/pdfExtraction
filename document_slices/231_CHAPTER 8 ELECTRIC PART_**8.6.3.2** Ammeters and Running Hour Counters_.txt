--------------------
Ammeters will be equipped on the starters for important motors in MSB and motor of 15 kW and above.  
The running hour counters will be equipped on the starters for important motors in MSB and motor of 7.5 kW and above.  
The ammeter on the starter shall be of overload type, the rated value shall be marked in red color.
