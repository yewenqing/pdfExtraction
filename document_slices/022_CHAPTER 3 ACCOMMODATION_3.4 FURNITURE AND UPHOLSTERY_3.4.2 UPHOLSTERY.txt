#### 3.4.2.1 Surface Material of Sofa and Chair  
Upholstery for sofa and chair shall be as follows:  
Woven fabric - Captain class cabins, Senior officer class cabins, Recreation room  
Vinyl leather: Other cabins and other public spaces.  
#### 3.4.2.2 Stuffing of Sofa, Chair and Mattress  
Upholstery of mattress: Cotton cloth  
Stuffing of mattress: Polyurethane sponge with coil spring  
Stuffing of sofa and chair: Polyurethane sponge
