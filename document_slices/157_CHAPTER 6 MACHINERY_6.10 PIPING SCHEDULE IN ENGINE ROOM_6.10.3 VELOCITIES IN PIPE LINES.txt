--------------------
The fluid velocities in pipelines shall be as per follows:  
Sea water cooling line, suction side: abt. 2.5m/s  
Sea water cooling line, discharge side: abt.3.5m/s  
Fresh cooling water line: abt.3m/s  
ballast and bilge line : abt. 3 m/s for steel pipes  
FO(excluding filling line): abt. 2.0m/s  
LO(excluding filling line): abt. 2.0m/s
