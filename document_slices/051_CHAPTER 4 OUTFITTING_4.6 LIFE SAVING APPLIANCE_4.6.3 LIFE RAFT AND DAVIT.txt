4.6.3.1 Life Raft  
Life rafts shall be provided as follow:  
|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| No. | Capacity (persons) | Type | Position |
| 1 | 24 | Inflatable, throw overboard type with a hydrostatic releasing device | Accommodation port side |
| 1 | 24 | Inflatable, throw overboard type with a hydrostatic releasing device | Accommodation starboard side |
| 2 | 6 | Inflatable, throw overboard type | Ship’s forward |  
Each one (1) embarkation ladder shall be supplied for liferaft at accommodation port side and starboard side.  
Two (2) embarkation ladders shall be arranged at the protected location on forward ship.  
The canvas covers for these ladders to be provided.  
The inflatable life rafts shall be stowed in fiber glass reinforced plastic (FRP) container. The container shall be stowed in cradle and float free,  
Effective term of nine (9) months shall be available for the life rafts at the time of the Vessel’s delivery.  
Indication lamp and instruction sheet (in English) shall be provided for life raft.  
4.6.3.2 Life Raft Davit  
Life raft davit shall not be provided.
