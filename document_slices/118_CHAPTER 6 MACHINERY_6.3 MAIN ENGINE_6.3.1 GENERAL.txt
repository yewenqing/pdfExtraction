The main engine shall be designed and manufactured under the license from MAN B&M.The particulars of main engine shall be as specified in Paragraph 6.2.1.  
The direction of engine revolution shall be clockwise, looking from aft.  
The main engine shall be remotely controlled from wheelhouse, navigation bridge deck wings and engine control room in the engine room.  
The main engine shall be temporarily controlled from local operating console. The main engine output shall be valid at following conditions:  
Atmospheric pressure: 0.1 MPa  
Ambient temperature in E/R: 45oC  
Sea water temperature: 32oC  
L.T. fresh water temperature: 36oC  
Relative humidity: 60%
