--------------------
In case of two or more sets of identical machinery units are installed, those machinery shall be distinguished by numbering. In general, the numbering shall be carried out in this way:  
From up to down  
From fore to aft.  
From port to starboard  
The measuring instruments used for equipment and piping shall be according to International Standard.  
Name plates shall be made of brass plate and marked by etching and painted black for illustration plate, red for warning plate and valve name plate respectively.
