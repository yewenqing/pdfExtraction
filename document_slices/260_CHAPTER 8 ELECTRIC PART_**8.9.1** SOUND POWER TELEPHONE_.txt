--------------------
One (1) set of selective calling type sound power telephone system shall be provided as follows:  
1- Wheelhouse  
1- Emergency generator room  
1- Fire control station  
1- Steering gear room  
1- Bow thruster room  
1- Engine control room/main engine side.  
1- Fuel oil filling station (one portable telephone and two sockets).  
The sound power telephone in engine room, steering gear room, bow thruster room and emergency generator room shall be of anti-noise type (head set type).
