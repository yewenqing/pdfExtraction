--------------------
One (1) set of gyro-compass shall be installed as follows:  
1 – Master compass  
1 – Digital repeater on W/H front ceiling console  
1 – Bearing repeater with pedestal on each bridge wings  
1 –Bearing repeater in steering stand of W/H  
1 – Repeater in steering gear room  
1 – Digital indication in captain’s day room  
1 – Course recorder (only heading signal to be recorded) in chart space of W/H  
1 – Rate of turn indicator on W/H front ceiling console  
1 – Set of azimuth mirror and azimuth circle  
1 – Repeater of heading type for conning position 1.of PANAMA required.(acc.rules)  
Heading output signals shall be sent to Inmarsat- Fleetboard Band if necessary, VDR, AIS, ECDIS, radars and auto-pilot etc. External signals from the speed log, DGPS etc. shall be received.  
The rate of turn signal shall sent to the ECDIS
