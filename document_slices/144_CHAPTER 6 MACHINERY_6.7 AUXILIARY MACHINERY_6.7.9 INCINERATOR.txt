--------------------
The incinerator shall be in compliance with the requirements of MARPOL 73/78 Annex VI and MEPC 76 (40) and alarms for furnace high temperature, flue high temperature, initiation failure, blanking failure and low fuel oil pressure etc.  
The incinerator shall be capable of burning solid waste and waste oil.  
Exhaust pipe for incinerator shall be provided with suitable expansion joints.  
The following accessories to be included:  
Burner unit 1 set  
Sludge oil circulating pump 1 set  
Diesel oil pump 1 set  
Sludge oil tank 1 set  
Flue gas fan 1set  
Flue gas damper 1 set  
Other necessary accessories, fittings, spares and tools shall be according to Maker's standard.
