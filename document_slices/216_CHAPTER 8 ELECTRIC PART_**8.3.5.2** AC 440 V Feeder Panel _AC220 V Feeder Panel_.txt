--------------------
Necessary quantity-Molded case circuit breaker (MCCB) or miniature circuit breaker (MCB) (with abt.10% spare breakers based on amount of circuit breakers on feed panel).  
Each panel:  
1 - Insulation monitor with alarm lamp (for AC 440V & 220V).  
1 - AC ammeter  
1 - Change-over switch for AC. ammeter (for each phase of emergency power transformer secondary circuit)  
1 - AC voltmeter  
1 - Change-over switch for AC voltmeter (between each phase of emergency power transformer secondary circuit)  
1 - Mega-ohm meter with change-over switch
