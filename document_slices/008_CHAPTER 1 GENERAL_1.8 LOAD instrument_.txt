One (1) set of loading computer with software approved by the class with certificate to be supplied by buyer.  
UPS to be supplied for loading computer. Loading computer with printer to be provided in shipâ€™s office.  
The calculation functions and display of the loading computer shall consist of:  
1. Displacement
2. Trim/draught (fore and aft)
3. Draft correction (fore and aft) due to difference of sea water density
4. Shear force and bending moment at the prescribed frame points
5. Maximum value of shear force and bending moment at the frame point
6. Intact stability calculation and check of damage stability(by minimum GM/ maximum KG)
7. Torsional strength
8. Summary of loading condition
9. Trim adjustment by ballast tank (program)
10. Navigation bridge visibility  
Lashing (if required):  
1. Container loading calculation by weight
2. Lashing strength and racking force calculation
3. Container control program  
Data of tank level to be automatically transmitted from the ballast and bunkering control system.
