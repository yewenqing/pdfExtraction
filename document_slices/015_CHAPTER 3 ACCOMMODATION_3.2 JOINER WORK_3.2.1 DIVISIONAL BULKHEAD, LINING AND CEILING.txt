The arrangement of all walls, bulkheads, lining and ceilings in living quarters shall be in accordance with Method “IC” as specified in SOLAS.  
Corridor bulkheads, except steel wall shall be constructed of “B” class fire retarding panel.  
Internal division bulkheads between cabins shall be “C” class panel. Non-combustible ceiling shall be provided in all cabins, public space and wheelhouse and “B” class non- combustible ceiling shall be provided in corridor of living space.  
Partition wall, lining and ceiling shall be according to Rule’s requirement.  
Watertight coaming with one side continuous welding to be applied for water-tightness in way of wet space and exterior steel boundary bulkhead.  
Wall panels to be jointed and installed shall be in accordance with the Maker’s standard. Other detail of securing and construction shall in accordance with the Builder’s practice, unless otherwise required by the Rule and Regulation.  
The Builder shall provide color samples for the Buyer’s selection of panel finishing.  
3.2.1.1 Partition Bulkhead  
The material and thickness of partition bulkhead to be as follows.  
|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Corridor bulkhead (“B” class) and division bulkhead | Rock wool insulation of 50 mm thickness furnished with galvanized steel sheet of 0.6 mm thickness decorated with PVC film on both sides. |
| Partition bulkhead between two adjacent cabins (without neighboring bed), cabin and public space | Rock wool insulation of 50mm thickness furnished with galvanized steel sheet of 0.6 mm thickness decorated with PVC film on both sides. |
| Partition bulkhead between two adjacent cabins with neighboring bed. | Double 25mm thick rock wool board with 30mm clearance, (the total thickness to be 80mm), Rock wool insulation of 25mm thickness furnished with galvanized steel sheet of 0.6 mm thickness decorated with PVC film on visible sides. |
