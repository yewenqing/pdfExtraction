Navigation equipment shall be fitted as follow:  
2 - Binocular, prism (marine type, 50mm x 7 times)  
1 - Aneroid barometer millibar scale  
1 - Thermometer for sea water  
**2 -** Thermometer for atmosphere (dry and wet)  
1 - Hygrometer  
1 - Hand lead, 3.2kg weight with 55 meters line  
1 - Deep sea lead, 12.8kg weight 220 meters line with reel  
1 - Chronometer, portable type (battery use)  
1 - Sextant  
2 - Clinometer (small clock type in wheelhouse and E/R)  
1 - Clinometer (in ship office)  
**Hygrometer and Barometer should have calibration certificates.**
