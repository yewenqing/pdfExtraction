--------------------
One whistle system with air horn, electric motor horn complete with automatic fog signal control unit shall be provided.  
Air horn with magnetic valve shall be mounted on the radar mast; electric motor horn shall be mounted on the fore mast; one (1) time controller shall be in the wheelhouse control console.  
Manual operation facility shall be provided for the air horn.  
The horn shall be capable of automatic blowing for fog signal by means of time controller; it may be operated by push button switches also as following:  
2- water tight type for wings;  
2- for bridge console near the OOW seat;  
2- at the front bulk of wheelhouse where the OOW usually stay for watching  
In addition, the air horn on the radar mast shall be operated by mechanical pull from the wheelhouse or one DC 24V solenoid valve to be provided for emergency operation purpose.  
The horn light may be used in common with Morse signal light also. And the light control function will meet requirement of IMO MSC.253 (83)  
Electric heaters for the air horn and electric motor horn shall be provided.
