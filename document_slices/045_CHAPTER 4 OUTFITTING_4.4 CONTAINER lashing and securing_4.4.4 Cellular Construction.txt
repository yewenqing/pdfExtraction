Cellular construction shall be of fixed, continuous type and designed to guide and support the six(6) tiers of 8’6”standard containers.  
The cell guide shall be reinforced to permit the stowage of 9’6” high cube container randomly in cargo hold in flexi loading condition.  
The cell guides shall be of about 160 mm x 160 mm x 14 mm equal angle construction. The cell guides shall extend continuously from the entry guide down to around 2.0 meters above the inner bottom or container platforms in the cargo hold.  
The top of each cell guide shall be connected to a fixed entry guide fabricated with ***22*** mm thickness steel plate, and the enter guides shall be of wave type in accordance with the Builder’s practice.  
The cell guides and cell guide support structure shall be designed based on the container unit weight of 30.5MT per 20ft/40ft container. ***From entry guide within 2 meters the support bracket shall be double pieces to be installed (to be confirm at approved drawing).***  
Side guide plates shall be provided in way of location between each 20ft container as follows:  
For guide plate in way of side longitudinal bulkhead of all cargo hold:  
(1) Approx. from hatch coaming top level to upper deck level  
(2) Approx. 1.5m length above the tank top 1000m.  
The side guide rails would be not fitted on other step bulkhead.  
The test for container loading and unloading with real or dummy container shall be carried out in accordance with the Builder’s practice.  
The bay numbers based on 40ft containers and row numbers shall be marked with Intermittent welding line and painted on the side/end wall of each cargo hold.
