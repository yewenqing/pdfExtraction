--------------------
All motors, unless otherwise specified, shall be marine type squirrel cage induction motors.  
Motors shall be designed for AC 440 V, 3 phase, 60 Hz, except for that the motors of 0.5 kW rating and less may be of AC 220 V, 3 phase or single phase type according to manufacturer's standard.  
Enclosure of motors shall be generally provided as follows:  
Water-proof type (IP 56) for motors exposed to weather.  
Totally enclosed type (IP 44) for steering gear, emergency fire pump and motors in engine room, provision refrigerating room, purifier area, galley and similar spaces.  
Drip-proof type (IP 23) used for motors in accommodation and other dry spaces.  
The enclosed type (IP23) will be for bow thruster motor.  
Space heater shall be provided for all motors on open deck and motors of steering gear, emergency fire pump, M/E turning gear, bow thruster and motor rated power over 10 kW in engine room.
