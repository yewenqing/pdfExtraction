--------------------
One (1) set of echo sounder with necessary accessories shall be installed as follows:  
1- Transducer in bottom of fwd working frequency type of transducer to be 200 kHz according to Maker’s standard,  
***(Option: 1- Transducer in bottom of stern, and the bridge screen at the same time can display the depth of F/A);***  
1- Main display in the chart space  
1- Display in wheelhouse ceiling panel.  
The maximum depth range scale is 300 meters or as per Maker’s standard and signal shall be transmitted to VDR and ECDIS.
