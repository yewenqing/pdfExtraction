--------------------
Telegraph system shall consist of engine telegraph, sub-engine telegraph and telegraph logger.  
1. Engine telegraph  
One (1) set of engine order telegraph system shall be provided, and it is to consist of:  
1 - transmitter with receiver with buzzer and illuminating lamp and dimmer in wheelhouse control console  
1 – transmitter (remote control lever) with buzzer and illuminating lamp and dimmer in each of navigation bridge deck wings  
1 – receiver / reply with bell in engine control console  
1 – receiver / with gong in engine side  
During wheelhouse control or navigation bridge deck wing control of main engine, the buzzer shall sound for abt. 2 seconds when the order partition is changed.  
2. Sub-engine telegraph  
One (1) set of sub-engine telegraph of push button with lamp type shall be provided in wheelhouse and engine control room.  
Partitions of the sub-telegraph shall be as follows:  
AT SEA  
STAND-BY  
FINISHED WITH ENGINE  
Audible devices of the sub-engine telegraph shall be one buzzer in each of wheelhouse and engine control room.  
3. Telegraph logger  
One (1) telegraph logger shall be provided in wheelhouse. The following items shall be recorded:  
Engine telegraph order & reply  
Sub-engine telegraph order & reply  
Time  
The recorded time shall be synchronous with Master clock.  
The achieved revolutions and time corresponding to the revolution  
Note: The M/E remote control system and telegraph system to be in accordance with rule requirement and may be changed according to final selected Maker’s standard.
