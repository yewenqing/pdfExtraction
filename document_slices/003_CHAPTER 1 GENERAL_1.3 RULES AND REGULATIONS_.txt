1.3.1 CLASSIFICATION  
The Vessel including its hull, machinery, equipment and outfitting shall be built under the supervision of Classification Society (hereinafter called the "Class") and shall be distinguished in the record by the notation of:  
***NK NS\* (CNC, EQ C DG, PSPC-WBT, NC) (IWS) (PSCM) (EA) (CSSA-R) (IHM) (Sox(EGCS)) MNS\*(M0)***  
***Installation Character(s): BWM, MPP, LSA, RCF, M0, AFS***  
***Loading computer will be approved by class.***  
However, the Class rule shall be applied with the latest version at the date of the Contract signing.  
1.3.2 FLAG  
The Vessel shall be registered under the flag of ***Marshall*** .
