--------------------
All purifiers shall be manually ON/OFF control on site operation and cleaning process –fully automated.  
Alarm and recirculation of oil feed to purifier upon loss of seal shall be provided for the purifier according to Maker's standard. Feed oil will be re-circulated by three way shifting valve, as per Maker’s standard.  
All oil purifiers shall be designed to have manual emergency stop function***, the emergency stop button shall be locate at the suitable place by the designing.***  
Running indication shall be in ECC. Alarm shall be in alarm and monitoring system (AMS). Auto. sludge discharging shall be performed.  
Hour counter for each purifier shall be provided or displayed in control board.
