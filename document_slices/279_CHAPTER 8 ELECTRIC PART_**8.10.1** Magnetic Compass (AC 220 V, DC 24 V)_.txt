--------------------
One set of transmitting type magnetic compass (TMC) with reflector binnacle shall be installed. Transmitting magnetic compass signal should be connected to auto pilot.  
Accessory to be provided as per the Maker’s standard.  
Heading signal of TMC should be sent to the steering unit for auto steering and off-course alarm in the event of a gyrocompass failure.  
One (1) spare bowl will be provided. Heading signal of TMC should be sent to the steering unit for auto steering and off-course alarm.  
A length of bypass tube and facilities shall be considered as per the maker’s standard if the binnacle had to locate a distance from the top of the helmsman position in the wheelhouse.  
Dimmer at bridge console for magnetic compass lamps of both DC24V and AC220V shall be provided.
