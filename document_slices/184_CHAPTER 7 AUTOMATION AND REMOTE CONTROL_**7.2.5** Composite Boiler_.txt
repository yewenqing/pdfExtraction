1. Automatic combustion control  
The fuel oil burning set for oil fired section of the composite boiler shall be provided with electric modulating controlling system.  
The burner shall be automatically lit and extinguished according to the boiler steam pressure.  
The burner shall be turned off automatically according to the rules. The manual stopping device shall be provided in ECC.  
2. Automatic control of surplus steam  
When the steam pressure generated by M/E exhaust gas section of the composite boiler is over the predetermined limit, the surplus steam shall be led to the atmospheric condenser automatically through a surplus steam dump valve.  
3. Automatic control of boiler water level  
Water level of the boiler shall be regulated automatically by level switch on the boiler.
