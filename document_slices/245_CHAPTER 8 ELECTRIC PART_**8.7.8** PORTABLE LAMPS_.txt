--------------------
Total ten (10) sets of 24 V, 60 W portable working lamps (no-explosion proof type) with protection cover shall be provided. Each portable lamp shall have flexible cord of fifteen (15) meters with plug.  
Five (5) sets of explosion proof type, chargeable, portable battery lamp shall be provided.
