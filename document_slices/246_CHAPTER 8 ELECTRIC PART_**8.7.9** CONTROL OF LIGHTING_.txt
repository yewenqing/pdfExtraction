--------------------
The ceiling lights in the engine room shall be generally controlled by miniature circuit breakers (MCB) of the distribution boards in the engine room.  
In mess rooms, recreation room, wheelhouse and engine control room etc. spaces, in case multiple access are arranged, the two-pole two-way switches shall be installed in each entrance for control the ceiling lights in common.  
The lights in workshops and engine & electrical stores shall be controlled by a switch located near the respective entrances.  
The lights in the machinery compartments such as steering gear room, emergency generator room, etc. shall be controlled by a switch located near respective entrances.  
Cargo hold lights shall be controlled by switches on main deck suitable location.  
**Cargo hold light (except inside C/H) and outside lights shall be controlled by switches in the wheelhouse.**  
The lights in the living quarters, public spaces, stores, etc. shall be controlled by respective switches located near the entrances in general.  
The lights in the inner passages shall be controlled by the circuit breakers of the distribution boards.  
The lights for the outside passages shall be controlled from the wheelhouse.  
The outside floodlights shall be controlled by respective switches on the group switch board in the wheelhouse.  
The lights in the stair case of the wheelhouse entrances shall be controlled by a door limit switch (if necessary) also.  
The lights of the explosion-proof type shall be controlled by respective switches located in the safety place.  
The lighting fixture in pipe duct shall be controlled (on/off) from the pipe duct entrance in engine room.  
**Emergency lights shall be arranged without local switches, except for wheelhouse and crew cabins.**
