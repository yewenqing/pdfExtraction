Indication of fire shall be provided on fire alarm panel in wheelhouse and extension alarm in fire control station in accordance with SOLAS.  
***Comply with latest FSS Code. E.g Detector and Control panel are to be tested according to EN54(2001)***  
Suitable numbers of fire/smoke detectors shall be provided in the machinery spaces, living quarters, alleyways, and staircase according to the requirement of the Rule/Regulations.  
Smoke tube type fire detecting system shall be provided for cargo holds.  
CO2 smoke suction line shall be led in each cargo hold, smoke detection with valve and suction fan shall be arranged in the CO2 bottle room, a repeater panel shall be in the wheel house.  
Audible / visual alarm shall be provided according to the Rule.  
Suitable numbers of push buttons shall be provided in access of every deck and passage ways and suitable spaces.  
The details of the system refer to “Chapter 9 - Electric Part” of the Specification.
