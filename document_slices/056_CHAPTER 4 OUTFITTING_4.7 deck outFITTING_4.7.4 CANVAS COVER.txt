--------------------
Canvas cover shall be provided for each of the following equipment on weather deck:  
* Chain pipe
* Winch drum of life boat davit
* Winch drum of accommodation ladder and pilot assistant ladder
* Pilot rope ladder
* Embarkation rope ladder for liferaft
* Magnetic compass and gyro repeater
* Search light
* Exposed flood light projector
