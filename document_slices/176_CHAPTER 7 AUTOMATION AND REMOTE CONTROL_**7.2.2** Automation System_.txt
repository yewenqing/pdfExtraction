--------------------
All pneumatic valves / items for main engine control should be tagged by stainless steel name plate with valve position deeply etched on it. This should exactly match with control drawing.  
Automation system consists of the following subsystems:  
1. DG Power Management System Refer to item 1-5.  
2. Automatic change-over of stand-by pumps  
The following pumps to be automatically changed-over by low pressure or low level and abnormal trip:  
- Cooling S.W. pump  
- M/E jacket cooling F.W. pump  
- L.T. cooling F.W. pump  
- Composite boiler feed water pump  
- F.O. pumps attached to the F.O. supply module unit  
- Composite boiler F.O. supply pump  
- Main L.O. pump  
3. Automatic regulation of temperature and viscosity  
The following equipment and systems to be provided with automatic regulation of temperature or/and viscosity.  
- HFO/LSHFO settling and service tanks (refer to Chapter 6 Machinery 6. 2.17)  
- Oil purifier’s heater (acc. to Maker’s recommendation)  
- F.O. supply module unit (acc. to Maker’s recommendation)  
- Generator engines (acc. to Maker’s recommendation)  
- Calorifier  
- M/E and G/E pre-heaters (acc. to Maker’s recommendation)  
- Following ancillary system for main engine (if necessary)  
M/E jacket cooling F.W. circuit  
Central F.W. coolers outlet  
M/E L.O. cooler outlet  
4. Ballast/bilge/fuel oil transfer pumps and valves/control system  
Refer to item 1-14.  
5. Automatic start and stop  
HFO/*LSHFO/*ULSFO transfer pump
