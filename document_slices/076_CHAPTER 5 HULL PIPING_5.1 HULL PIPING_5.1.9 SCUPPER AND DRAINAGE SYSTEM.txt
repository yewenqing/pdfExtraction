Sufficient scuppers shall be provided for all decks, top of deck houses and other enclosed spaces, wherever necessary.  
Drainage shall be arranged away from accommodation ladder, pilot ladder and life boat position.  
Upper deck scuppers shall have \grids, a rubber plug shall be provided on the top of each scupper.  
Separate scuppers for refrigerated space shall be with plugs.  
Scupper pipes shall be led to the weather decks or overboard directly according to the situation.  
Storm valves shall be fitted for necessary pipes as required by the Classification Society.  
Water sealed traps shall be fitted to the refrigerated provision chambers.  
Scuppers shall be arranged accessibly, and hard bending for galley scuppers and soil pipes shall be avoided as far as possible.  
Gutter way in stainless steel shall be arranged on the deck of galley with scuppers on each corner. Fat trap shall be provided for galley scuppers.  
Adequate cleaning plugs shall be fitted, wherever necessary.  
Necessary drain plugs shall be fitted as required.  
Drain pan shall be fitted frames.  
Gutter bar shall be fitted along the inside deck house on each deck, finally according to the detail design.  
Gutter bars shall be fitted around deck machinery, steering gear etc.  
Drain pipe from funnel top to engine casing top shall be provided.
