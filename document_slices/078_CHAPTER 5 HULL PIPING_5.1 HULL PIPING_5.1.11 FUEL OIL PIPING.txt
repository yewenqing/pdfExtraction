Fuel oil filling main lines of one (1) DN200 for HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO and one(1) DN100 for MGO respectively with butterfly valve and blank flange shall be provided.  
The remote control valve shall be fitted on branch fuel oil filling line of each HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO bunker tanks.  
The main lines shall be connected to the fuel oil fill piping and fuel oil transfer pump in engine room.  
Each one(1) bunker station for HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO and MGO shall be provided at each side of the vessel on upper deck, and with sampling device, thermometer, butterfly valves blank flanges, pressure gauges, spill trays etc.  
Oil spill spaces with drain plug shall be provided at F.O. filling stations.  
Caution plate required by U.S.C.G. shall be fitted on bunker station and ship office.  
Remote level reading for oil tanks shall be indicated in engine room.  
High level alarms shall be provided for HFO/ULSFO bunker tanks and MDO/MGO bunker tank.
