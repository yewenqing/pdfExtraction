--------------------
Portable form, CO2 and/or dry power fire extinguishers shall be furnished and properly secured in accordance with the requirement of the SOLAS.  
|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Type | Space |
| 5 kg CO2 type | Wheel house & chart space, electric equipment room, electrical workshop, galley, ER workshop, emergency generator room, engine room, air conditioning room, ship office and fire control station |
| 45L foam type | Engine room |
| 135L foam type | Engine room |
| 9L foam type or 6 kg dry powder type | Space where required by rules |  
The number of portable fire extinguishers shall be decided in accordance with the rule requirement. Spare charges shall be stowed in the safety store in living quarters.  
Survey card shall be fitted for each portable fire extinguisher.
