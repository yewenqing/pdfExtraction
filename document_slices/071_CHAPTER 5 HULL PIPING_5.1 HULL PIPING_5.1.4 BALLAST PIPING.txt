One (1) ballast main ring line shall be arranged in pipe duct for ballasting, de-ballasting and transferring the ballast water in double bottom ballast tanks, side ballast tanks.  
Ballast system shall consist of two (2) ballast pumps and one (1) ballast water treatment plant in engine room.  
Trim adjustment shall be remotely done by ballast system from ship office.  
A suction bell mouth shall be provided in each ballast tank. The suction mouth shall be connected with flange in tanks and easily dismounted.  
Drain hole shall be provided enough at structure member surrounding mouth pieces.  
The remote control pneumatic butterfly valve shall be fitted on each branch ballast line in pipe duct and shall be remote operated from ship office.  
All remote operated ballast water valves can be open/shut manually in case failure of remote operation. And local open/shut indication shall be provided for the remote operated ballast water valves.  
The valve before collision bulkhead shall be fitted directly at the collision bulkhead inside the bow thruster & emergency fire pump room located after the collision bulkhead, the valve operation position as per Class Rules.  
Remote level able to display tank volumes and draft reading for ballast tanks shall be indicated on monitor in ship office and loading computer.  
Particulars of ballast pumps and remote control butterfly valve system refer to Machinery Chapter of the specification.
