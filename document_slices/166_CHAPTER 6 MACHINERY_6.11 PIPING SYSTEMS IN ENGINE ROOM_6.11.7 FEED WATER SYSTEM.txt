Condensate from atmos. condenser shall be discharged to cascade tank by gravity.  
Feed water pump of the composite boiler shall draw water from the cascade tank and then discharge it to the composite boiler.  
Feed water to cascade tank shall be manually from the F.W. hydrophore.  
The bottom and surface blow down lines of the composite boiler shall be combined to led overboard.  
The blow-down lines shall have double valves.  
Each feed water pump shall have a capacity for max. evaporation when the main engine operating at SMCR and one (1) pump of them shall serve as stand-by.
