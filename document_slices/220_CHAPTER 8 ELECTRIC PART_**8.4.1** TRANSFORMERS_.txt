1. For General Service  
Two (2) sets of 3 phase main transformer (i.e. d-d or y-y) shall be installed in the engine room. One (1) transformer shall be capable of supplying to all AC220V service so that only one (1) transformer is for working and the other is for spare. Working transformer manually changing-over shall be provided only.  
Principal particulars shall be as follows:  
Type: Drip-proof, dry type  
Output: Abt.99 kVA (as per final E-balance)  
Number: 2 (one as stand-by)  
Voltage: AC 450/230V  
Frequency: 60 Hz  
Insulation: Class F  
Rating: Continuous  
Cooling: Natural-cooling  
1. For Emergency Service  
Two (2) sets of 3 phase transformers (i.e. d-y11) shall be installed in the emergency generator room for emergency service. One (1) transformer shall be capable of supplying all emergency service so that only one (1) transformer is for working and the other is for spare. Working transformer manually changing-over shall be provided.  
Principal particulars shall be as follows:  
Type: Drip-proof, dry type  
Output: Abt. 50 kVA (as per final E-balance)  
Number: 2 (one as stand-by)  
Voltage: AC 450/230 V  
Frequency: 60 Hz  
Insulation: Class F  
Rating: Continuous  
Cooling: Natural-cooling  
1. For Galley Service  
One (1) set of 3 phase transformers (i.e. y-d11) shall be installed in galley room or near place for galley service. The transformer shall be double wiring and capable of supplying all AC440V & AC220Vservice.  
Principal particulars shall be as follows:  
Type: Drip-proof, dry type  
Output: Abt. 99 kVA (450V 65kVA / 230V 30kVA) (as per final E-balance)  
Number: 1  
Voltage: AC 450/450V/ 230 V  
Frequency: 60 Hz  
Insulation: Class F  
Rating: Continuous  
Cooling: Natural-cooling
