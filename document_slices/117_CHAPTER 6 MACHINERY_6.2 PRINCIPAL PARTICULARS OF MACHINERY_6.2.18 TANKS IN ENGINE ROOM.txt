Notes:  
(1) The final capacity for some pumps and aux. equipment serving M/E, G/E and boiler and so onsuch as cooling water pump, and plate cooler etc. may be adjusted after detail heat balance.  
(2) The final capacity for the tanks may be adjusted after determination of hull line and position of the tank.  
(3) The total pressure for screw pump and gear pump to be contained 0.05MPa suction head.  
(4) AT with mark “\*” to be arranged for HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO bunker tanks within cargo hold area.  
(5) The HFO tanks with mark “\*\*” to be meant the tanks to have inclined bottom.  
(6) All tanks to be made of welded steel plate and well secured to the hull. Each tank shall be provided with necessary fittings such as inlet and outlet connections, drain, air vent, manhole or hand-hole, overflow, thermometer socket, steam blow-out connection, etc. all depending on the purposes for which they are intended.  
(7) The M/E L.O. sump tank shall be fitted with heating coil only around the suction bell mouth.  
(8) All of the oil tanks shall be provided with drip tray, as practicable.
