Following system shall be provided

Steam shall be supplied to various services at following pressure:  
0.7 MPa: HFO and LSHFO/ULSFO service and settling tanks heating  
HFO,LSHFO/ULSFO heater for F.O. supply module unit  
HFO,LSHFO/ULSFO &LO heaters for purifiers  
Steam tracing  
Other tank heating and heaters (in engine room)  
0.4MPa: Calorifier  
Air conditioning plant  
Sea chests in engine room  
Miscellaneous usage  
Drain from HFO,ULHFO/LSHFO and lub. oil tank heating coil and oil heaters shall be led to the cascade tank through atmos. condenser and the inspection tank.  
Drain from the calorifier and accommodation also shall be led to the cascade tank.  
Drain traps with by-pass and check cock shall be provided for HFO,LSHFO/ULSFO and lub. Oil tankâ€™s coil and oil heaters.  
Escape lines for steam safety/relief valves shall be led to the funnel.  
Flange type or screw type steam hose connections shall be provided for cleaning oil tanks, sea chest etc. in the engine room.  
No shore steam connection shall be provided.  
The inspection tank shall be integrated with cascade tank or separately arranged.  
Excess steam from M/E exhaust gas section of the composite boiler shall be led to cascade tank through auto. steam dump and atmos. condenser.
