--------------------
Exhaust gas from the turbocharger of the main engine shall be led to the M/E exhaust gas section of the composite boiler , scrubber unit and discharged directly to open air.  
No exhaust gas by-pass line to be provided for exhaust gas section of the composite boiler.  
The oil fired section and incinerator shall be led up independently to open air.  
Exhaust gas from G/E sets engine shall be led up independently to scrubber unit through each silencer.  
Stainless steel expansion elements for exhaust gas pipes of main and gen. engines shall be fitted immediately after turbocharger through transition connection.  
Adequate supports shall be fitted so that the weight of the piping shall not rest on the turbocharger, boiler, incinerator, silencers and expansion elements, etc.  
***Recommend amend to Exhaust gas uptakes in the funnel to be insulated up to about at least 1,000 mm from the top plate of funnel.***  
Necessary drains from the exhaust gas pipes and silencers shall be arranged with drain traps and led to bilge well.
