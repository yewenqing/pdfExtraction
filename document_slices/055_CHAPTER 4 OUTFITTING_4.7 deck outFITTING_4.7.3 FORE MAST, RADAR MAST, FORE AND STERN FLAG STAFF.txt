Fore mast to be of fabricated steel construction, and to be arranged on the forecastle deck at the center line of the Vessel.  
The fore mast to be furnished with vertical ladders, back hoop, mast light. Necessary fittings and eye plates for steel wire rope stays to be fitted to minimize vibration.  
Radar mast to be of fabricated steel and to be arranged on the compass deck at the center line of the Vessel.  
The radar mast shall be connected with under deck structure effectively and fastened by rigging and tackles to minimize vibration.  
The radar mast to be furnished with vertical ladder, back hook, radar scanner, antenna fittings, mast light, signal yard, signal lamp. Necessary fittings and eye plates for steel wire rope stays to be fitted to minimize vibration.  
Antenna post to be of steel pipe construction of self -standing type.  
Necessary rigging and tackles for davits, flag yards, signal yard, etc. to be furnished completely.  
All riggings to be greased before delivery and covered with vinyl cover.  
One (1) ensign staff to be fitted at stern and one (1) jack staff at bow. And anchor lamp to be provided. Rigging to be fitted for flag staff completely.  
One (1) flag box to be place around the fore and stern flag staff.  
**Cables installed on mast or post on upper deck to be wired in galvanized steel pipe up to about 300mm height from the deck.**
