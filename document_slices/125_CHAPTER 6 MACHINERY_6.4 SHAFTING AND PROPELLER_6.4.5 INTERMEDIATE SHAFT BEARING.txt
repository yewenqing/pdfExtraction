--------------------
The intermediate shaft bearing shall be of the single oil disc type and of cast iron or cast steel pedestal, upper and lower half shells lined with white metal.  
The bearing shall be provided with ample oil reservoir and cooling water jacket inside the pedestal, with end baffle to prevent loss of oil at both ends of bearing.  
The bearing shall be central LT fresh-water cooled. And a bearing temperature sensor shall be provided and connected to ECR.  
For checking the oil levels in the bearing housing, the bearing shall be fitted with a sounding bar.  
The number of bearing may be adjusted at detail design stage as per final shaft alignment.
