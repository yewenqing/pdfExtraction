--------------------
One (1) set of electrical rudder angle indicator system shall be provided as follows:  
* One (1) transmitter with link bar stick in the steering gear room
* One (1) three (3) face type indicator in the wheelhouse
* One (1) indicator on wheelhouse ceiling console
* One (1) indicator in the engine control console
* Each one (1) indicator of watertight type on both bridge wings
* One (1) indicator in the steering gear room
* One (1) indicator on the navigation control console  
Illumination with dimmer switch shall be provided for the rudder angle indicators in the wheelhouse and bridge wings.
