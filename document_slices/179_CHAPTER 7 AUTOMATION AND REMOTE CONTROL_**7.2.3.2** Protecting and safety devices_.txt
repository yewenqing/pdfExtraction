--------------------
a. Emergency trip  
Main engine shall be automatically tripped with an alarm according to Rules and propulsion plant manufacturer's requirements.  
Each one manual emergency trip push button with cover shall be provided in engine control console, local emergency control station, navigation bridge deck wings and W/H control console.  
Emergency trip system shall be independent of the remote control system and separated from Engineâ€™s alarm and monitoring system.  
Reset of trip circuit shall be carried out by setting the maneuvering/telegraph lever in engine control room at stop position after recovering to normal condition.  
b. Critical speed protection  
Automatic quick passing program shall be applied over the critical revolution zone during bridge control (if any).  
Critical speed alarm to be provided in AMS ***for the long time running***.  
c. Program speed control  
When controlled in wheelhouse, the engine speed shall be controlled by load-up / load down program over certain ahead speed by program device with readjusted time up to the set speed of the maneuvering / telegraph lever.  
One override push button for program speed up shall be provided in each of wheelhouse control console and navigation bridge deck wings.  
d. Turning interlock  
While the turning gear is engaged, a starting pilot air valve for starting shall be locked.  
e. Automatic slow-down  
The M/E speed shall be automatically reduced according to Rules and propulsion plant Manufacturer's requirements.  
One by-pass push button of this system shall be provided in each of W/H control console and navigation bridge deck wings or as per maker standard.  
Reset of the automatic slow down circuit shall be carried out by setting the telegraph lever in selected main engine control position below a predetermined position after recovering to normal condition.  
Alarm and separate indicator for above as well as emergency trip shall be provided on engine control console in engine control room and W/H control console.
