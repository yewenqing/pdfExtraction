--------------------
The following auxiliaries to be operated in local automatic system:  
* Fresh water generator abnormal alarm shall be connected to AMS and high salinity alarm in monitoring system.
* Salinometer shall be provided with automatic control to re-circulate distillate on high salinity.
* F. W. pumps shall be automatically operated in dependence on the tanks pressure
* Oily bilge separator with failure/15ppm alarm in alarm and monitoring system (AMS).
* Incinerator with failure alarm in alarm and monitoring system (AMS).
* Sewage treatment plant with failure alarm in alarm and monitoring system (AMS).
* Ballast water treatment plant with failure alarm in alarm and monitoring system and remote control from ships office.
