--------------------
Abt. ***Three(3)*** sets of window wipers with heater ***and Two(2) CVS*** shall be installed on the front windows of the wheelhouse according to rules requirements. (Refer to Accommodation Part)  
The wiper shall be of horizontal movement type. Speed controller at the bridge console shall be provided for each window wiper.  
***Each CVS controller shall be located at the lower part of front bulkhead***
