#### 4.7.2.1 Accommodation ladder  
|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| No. of set | Two (2) |
| Location | Accommodation sides on upper deck |
| Type | One (1) man operated self-stowing, vertical stowing, one (1) piece type |
| Step | Fixed non-slip curved step |
| Material | Al-alloy for frame, step, rail and stanchion  Galvanized steel for upper platform and turn table |
| Clear breadth | Approx. 600mm |
| Davit | One (1) davit and one (1) winch with galvanized steel wire rope rigging for each accommodation ladder |
|  |  |
| Motor | One (1) set of fixed electric motor for each accommodation ladder |
| Accessories | Rail and stanchions of hinged type, safety net, lower/upper platform and protection rollers |
| Control | From the control stand on the upper deck near each accommodation ladder |  
Ladder shall have length to reach not more than 600 mm above the light ballast water line at an inclination of 55 deg. to horizontal.  
Ladder shall be stowed vertically within the Vesselâ€™s side line on upper deck.  
The SWL shall be marked with paint. ***Must be marked with the maximum load the weights and number of persons on the ladder at the entrance and exit.***  
***Secondary support for accommodation ladder shall be provided.***  
#### 4.7.2.2 Pilot Rope Ladder  
Two (2) sets of pilot rope ladder with wooden steps shall be provided. ***Pilot rope ladder to be confirmed that the ISO or Class certificate is attached***
