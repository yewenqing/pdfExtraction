--------------------
A Manoeuvring/Morse signal lamp shall be mounted on the top of radar mast and four (4) keys shall be located at both bridge wings and on front wall of wheelhouse.  
The Manoeuvring/Morse signal lamp shall also be controlled by the ship's horn system.  
The control method of the Morse & manoeuvreing signal lamp should also meet the performance standard requirement MSC.253 (83) and as per Rules.  
**The source of maneuvering/morse signal lights shall from navigation light control panel.**
