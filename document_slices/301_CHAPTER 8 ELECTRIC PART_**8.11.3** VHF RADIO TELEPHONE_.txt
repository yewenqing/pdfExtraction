--------------------
Two (2) sets of VHF radiotelephone of DSC with 57 channels and USA channel shall be installed in wheelhouse control console.  
Each set including:  
1-VHF telephone with DSC and whip antenna  
1-VHF DSC watch receiver (channel 70) with antenna  
The No.1 VHF shall be installed at Navigation Signal Console in hands reach area near the OOW seat, with one(1) remote control handset at the front bulk of wheelhouse.  
No.2 VHF shall be installed at Navigation Console in hands reach area near the OOW seat, with slave sockets for both wings .  
One (1) portable handset will be provided for both wings.  
DC 24V Power should be fed from the radio battery.
