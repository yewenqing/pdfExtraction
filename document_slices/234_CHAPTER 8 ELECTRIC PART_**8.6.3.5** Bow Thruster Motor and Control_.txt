--------------------
Bow thruster motor shall be interlocked with bow thruster room fan and the pitch of blades is in zero position.  
The motor shall be of single speed and non-reversible type. The motor rating is S2-30 min as per manufacturerâ€™s manual.  
The motor can be started/stopped from local starter and wheelhouse and can be emergency stopped from both bridge wings. The running indication shall be provided in wheelhouse.  
PT100 temperature sensors will be supplied in main motor winding.  
The starter for bow thruster is located in bow thruster room or suitable place,**BT starter to be in upper deck level of BT room and not in the same level as BT motor..**  
Bow thruster pitch order & response signal will be supplied to VDR system from bow thruster remote control system.  
Adjustable timer shall be provided in the starter to prevent immediate re-starting of bow thruster.  
**Load indicator to be provided in wheel house panel. MSB Bow Thruster Feeder Panel to include Running indication and Abnormal Condition Lights.**  
Following shall be provided for the control of bow thruster.
