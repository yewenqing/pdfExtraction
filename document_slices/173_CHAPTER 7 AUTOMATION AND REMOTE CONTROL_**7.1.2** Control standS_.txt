The following control stands shall be provided:  
- Wheelhouse control console (WHC) including Bridge Maneuvering Console (BMC) and Navigation Signal Console (NSC) in wheelhouse  
- Engine control room console (ECC) in engine control room.  
- Ballast control station in ship office.  
- Local stands for local controls of machines.  
- Bridge wing control console at navigation bridge deck wings.  
1. Bridge maneuvering console  
Refer to “Chapter 8 Electric”.  
2. Engine control room console  
The console shall be of steel construction self-standing type, with hinged doors in front side and removable doors in rear side if applicable.  
The console shall be supplied from main switchboard and emergency switchboard by means of each one feeder 3 x 220V AC, and from charging & discharging board by means of one feeder 24V DC.  
Inside the console AC220 V socket outlets shall be provided.  
Inside the console 2 pcs of AC220V 20W lights should be provided.  
The following equipment shall be included in/on the console, but not limit to:  
*-* Main propulsion–main operation panel / safety panel  
- Main engine indication panel  
- Engine telegraph (receiver / transmitter)  
- 2 pcs of PC workstation with 24 inch TFT VDU and other engine room monitoring and alarm equipment  
- Alarm & Data logging printer  
- Slave clock  
- Dead man alarm main switch or push button (combined in extension alarm panel)  
- Remote water level analogue or digital indicator for boiler  
- Main engine auxiliary blower, and start / stop switch with running indication lampas per main engine Maker’s standard  
- Composite boiler emergency stop push button  
- Composite boiler running (firing on) lamp  
- Composite boiler steam pressure gauge  
- Main engine RPM indicator and revolution counter  
- Main engine turbocharger RPM indicator  
* Scrubber monitoring and shut-down control in emergency  
- Rudder angle indicator  
- Analogue measuring instruments for essential pressures and temperatures  
- Main air compressor auto/start/stop switch and running indication  
- Fuel oil viscosity indication  
- HFO, LSHFO and MGO change-over position indicator  
- Duty engineer selector  
- Auto. telephone  
- Speed log indicator  
- Sound power telephone  
- Suitable place for writing  
- Lubricator controller .  
- Temperature controllers of HT, LT & LO.  
- M/E running hour counter  
Water mist alarm repeater panel  
Manual emergency trip buttons with cover of necessary  
- Other necessary equipment specified elsewhere  
3. Ballast control station in ship office. Refer to item 1-14.  
4. Local stand for local control of main engine. Refer to “Chapter 6 Machinery”.  
5. Bridge wing console at navigation bridge deck wing. Refer to “Chapter 8 – Electric”.
