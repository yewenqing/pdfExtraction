--------------------
The following navigation lights shall be of double lamp type and connected to a panel with control switches and indicating lamps graphically arranged, audible and visible signal alarm, and this panel shall be incorporated into the wheelhouse control console.  
Navigation lamps should be installed according the requirement of Rules. Navigation lights and its controller should meet the performance standard requirement MSC.253 (83) and as per rules.  
Two separate feeder circuits shall be provided for the panel, one (1) from the AC 220V feeder panel of main switchboard directly and one (1) from the AC 220 V feeder panel of emergency switchboard directly.  
Two (2) - Mast head lights AC. 220 V, 60-65 W  
Two (2) - Side lights AC. 220 V, 60-65 W  
One (1) - Stern light AC. 220 V, 60-65 W
