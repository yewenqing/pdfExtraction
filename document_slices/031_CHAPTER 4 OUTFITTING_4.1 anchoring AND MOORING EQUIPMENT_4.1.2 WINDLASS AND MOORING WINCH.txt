#### 4.1.2.1 Combined Windlass/Mooring Winch  
Two (2) sets of combined windlass/mooring winch of independent type shall be located at forecastle deck.  
Each combined windlass/mooring winch shall be composed of, one (1) declutchable cable lifter, two(2) declutchable mooring drum and one (1) warping head.  
Gypsy wheel and mooring drum shall not be operated simultaneously.  
The mooring drum shall be designed to take one whole rope on six (6) or seven (7) layers, declutched to the shaft and operated through clutch.  
Brake holding force of windlass shall be sufficient in accordance with the requirements of Classification Society.  
***It has to be ensured that all flanges of the hydraulic pipes at the pumping station and on deck near the windlass and mooring winches fall with in the oil coaming.***  
Particulars of each combined windlass/mooring winch shall be as follows.  
|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Item | Gypsy | Mooring drum | Warping head |
| Quantity | 1 | 2 | 1 |
| Capacity | A rated line pull in compliance with the requirement of the Classification Society at average hoisting speed of about 9m/min | per drum abt.15tx15m/min  (on the first layer) | abt.15t |
| Standard for chain, rope and capacity | Φ70, Grade 3 chain of welding. Joining shackles shall be of Kenter type | Φ64x ***220m*** rope (size subject to maker’s recommendation)  With min. strength abt.638KN | six(6) turns |
