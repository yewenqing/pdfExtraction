--------------------
One (1) set fire alarm control panel of local water mist system shall be in the fire control station.  
One (1) set local pumps unit and starters in E/R.  
One (1) set repeater panel in wheelhouse control console and alarms connected to the AMS  
Other flame detector, manually operated call points, flash lamp & sirens etc. will be provided in each local mist area (i.e. M/E, A/E, F.O. purifier, boiler area etc.) as per rule.
