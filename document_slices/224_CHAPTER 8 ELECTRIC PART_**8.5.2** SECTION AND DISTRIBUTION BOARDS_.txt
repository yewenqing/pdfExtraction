--------------------
All section boards and distribution boards shall be enclosed with steel sheet case.  
***Certified rubber insulation mats (IEC61111) shall be provided around reefer container distribution panels.***  
Distribution boards and section boards fitted in the living quarters and other similar places shall be of non-water-proof type, and those fitted in the engine room, steering gear room, stores and other similar places shall be of drip-proof type.  
Reefer container distribution panels shall be fitted in under deck passageway of cargo hold region and shall be non-water-proof type.  
In general, distribution boards and section boards shall be equipped with two or three pole miniature circuit breaker (MCB).  
An electric circuit drawing shall be provided in a vinyl envelope in each distribution boards or boxes.  
The circuit label shall be fitted on the inside of each board door.
