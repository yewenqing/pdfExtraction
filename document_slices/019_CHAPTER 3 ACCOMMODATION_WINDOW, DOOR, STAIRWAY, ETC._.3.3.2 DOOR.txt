The weather tight door for emergency escape trunk shall be of quick-open type.

#### 3.3.2.3 Hardware  
All hardware shall be of marine type.  
##### 3.3.2.3.1 Lock  
All door handles shall be of lever type，***the springs to be moderately heavy type.***  
All locks and latches shall have rim or mortise type casing to meet the requirements of particular installations.  
Locks shall be furnished so that each lock will have a different key, and all locks except lavatory locks can be opened by a master key.  
All living cabins shall have mortise type cylinder lock and latch set with thumb turn.  
Public space, catering space, wheelhouse, fire control station, engine control room, laundry, changing room and inside lockers shall have mortise type cylinder lock and latch set.  
Weather doors on A to F deck and wheelhouse to have mortise lock.  
Public/deck toilet and hospital bathroom door shall have mortise type latch with "vacant" indicator. Bonded store door shall have padlock made of brass.  
Weather deck steel doors shall have latch， padlock or cylinder lock where necessary.  
General Anti-pirate locking device to be provided for all external doors.  
Locking device only openable from inside shall be fitted at steel doors to accommodation area and engine room area from upper deck as per ISPS.  
##### 3.3.2.3.2 Key  
Three (3) sets of key shall be supplied for each lock set except "vacant" lock.  
Keys shall be divided into user’s group in accordance with shipboard practice.  
Key of each door to be different, doors of one room to use the same key.  
Three (3) sets of master key shall be provided.  
All keys shall be supplied with a plastic identification tag. A list of keys identifying the cabin and number shall be supplied by the Builder.  
The tag shall be marked in English only.  
##### 3.3.2.3.3 Door Closer  
Door self-closing device shall be provided for the following locations except steel doors :  
* + - * Engine room entrance
* Stairway
* Electric cable trunk, A.C. ventilation trunk and pipe tunnel in accommodation block
* Public toilet
* Inside passage entrance door on upper deck, A deck and B deck
* Engine control room
* Galley
* Wheel house
* A-class, B-class fire doors (if required by Class rules)  
Door closers shall be of sufficient size to close the doors under all conditions of list and trim.
